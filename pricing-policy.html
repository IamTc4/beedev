DeveloperBee Pricing & Service Policies

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

These policies ensure transparent communication, predictable costs, and smooth project delivery for all clients. By engaging with DeveloperBee's services, you agree to these terms.

⭐ 1. Pricing Structure

DeveloperBee follows a project-based + module-based pricing model tailored to your specific needs.

1️⃣ Fixed Price Projects

Used for:

  • Websites
  • E-commerce platforms
  • Dashboards
  • Automation tools
  • Branding packages

A fixed quote is provided after comprehensive requirement analysis.

2️⃣ Hourly / Task-Based Pricing

Used for:

  • Small changes and updates
  • Bug fixes
  • Feature upgrades
  • Media editing
  • Social media posts

3️⃣ Monthly Retainers

Used for:

  • Social media management
  • Maintenance & updates
  • Ongoing automation support
  • Dashboard monitoring
  • Security audits

⭐ 2. Payment Terms

To ensure smooth project execution and resource allocation, we follow a milestone-based payment structure:

✔ 50% Advance Payment (Non-Refundable)

Required for project kickoff and resource allocation. This secures your project slot and allows us to begin work immediately.

✔ 30% Mid-Project Payment

Due after design approval or first demo presentation. This milestone confirms we're aligned with your vision.

✔ 20% Before Final Delivery

Required before handing over full files, code, or deployment. This ensures all requirements are met before final handover.

Accepted Payment Methods: UPI, Bank Transfer, PayPal, Stripe, Razorpay

⭐ 3. Revision Policy

To ensure clarity and avoid unlimited revision cycles, we include a specific number of revisions based on project size:

  • Small Projects: 2–3 revisions included
  • Medium Projects: 4 revisions included
  • Large Projects: Up to 6 revisions included

Extra revisions are chargeable based on complexity.

Revisions Do NOT Include:

  • New pages or sections
  • New features or functionality
  • Scope changes or additions
  • Complete brand redesign
  • Additional modules or integrations

⭐ 4. Scope Policy (Very Important)

All project scope is finalized and documented before starting work. This includes:

  • Number of pages/screens
  • Features and functionality
  • Integrations required
  • Design complexity
  • Timeline expectations

Important: Anything outside the agreed scope is considered either a new task or a paid add-on. This prevents delays and ensures clean project flow.

⭐ 5. Refund Policy

We maintain a clear refund policy to build trust and set proper expectations.

❌ No Refund For:

  • Advance payment (project kickoff fee)
  • Completed milestones and delivered work
  • Work already delivered and approved
  • Change of mind by the client
  • Work delays due to missing client inputs or late responses

✔ Partial Refund Available:

Only if DeveloperBee fails to start or deliver the first milestone within the agreed timeline (rare case). Refund amount will be proportional to work not completed.

⭐ 6. Delivery & Timeline Policy

Delivery timelines depend on project size, client response time, and feature complexity.

Expected Turnaround Times:

  • Landing Page: 3–5 days
  • Website: 1–3 weeks
  • E-commerce: 2–4 weeks
  • Automation: 3–10 days
  • Dashboard: 5–12 days
  • Branding/Media: 2–7 days

Note: Delays caused by late client responses, missing content, or requirement changes will extend timelines automatically.

⭐ 7. Maintenance Policy

We provide complimentary maintenance for a limited period after project delivery.

Free Maintenance Period:

✔ 15–30 days after delivery (depending on project size)

Free Maintenance Covers:

  • Minor bug fixes
  • Small layout adjustments
  • Bug corrections
  • Performance tweaks

NOT Covered in Free Maintenance:

  • New features or functionality
  • Complete redesign
  • New automation rules
  • Content uploading and management
  • Server or hosting issues

Paid monthly maintenance packages are available for ongoing support.

⭐ 8. Support Policy

DeveloperBee provides multiple support channels for your convenience:

  • ✔ Live chat support
  • ✔ WhatsApp support
  • ✔ Email support
  • ✔ Priority support (paid plans)

Support Hours: Monday–Saturday, 10 AM – 7 PM IST

Emergency support available for critical issues on paid maintenance plans.

⭐ 9. Intellectual Property Policy

We believe in complete transparency regarding ownership rights.

After Final Payment:

  • Client receives full ownership of all code, designs, dashboards, automations, and media created for the project
  • All source files and documentation are transferred
  • No ongoing licensing fees or restrictions

Portfolio Rights:

DeveloperBee maintains the right to showcase completed projects in our portfolio unless a confidentiality agreement (NDA) is signed. We respect client privacy and will not disclose sensitive business information.

⭐ 10. Deployment Policy

DeveloperBee handles complete deployment and setup as part of our service:

Included in Deployment:

  • Website deployment to hosting
  • Hosting setup and configuration
  • DNS configuration
  • SSL certificate activation
  • Basic speed optimization
  • Initial testing and quality checks

Note: Advanced optimization (CDN setup, advanced caching, image optimization) may be charged separately based on requirements.

⭐ 11. Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances change. Here's how cancellations are handled:

If Client Cancels:

  • Work completed will be charged proportionally
  • Advance payment is non-refundable
  • All work pauses immediately upon cancellation notice
  • Completed work files will be delivered upon settlement

If DeveloperBee Cancels:

  • Partial refund based on work not completed
  • All completed work files handed over professionally
  • Assistance in transitioning to another provider if needed

⭐ 12. Confidentiality & NDA

We take your privacy and business confidentiality seriously.

NDA Available Upon Request For:

  • Startup ideas and concepts
  • Proprietary automation systems
  • Sensitive data dashboards
  • AI logic and algorithms
  • High-security projects
  • Competitive business strategies

Standard confidentiality is maintained for all projects. Formal NDA can be signed before project kickoff for additional legal protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I negotiate the payment terms?

For large projects (above ₹5 lakhs), we can discuss customized payment milestones. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What happens if I need changes after the free maintenance period?

You can opt for our monthly maintenance package or request changes on a per-task basis with hourly billing.

Do you offer refunds for unsatisfactory work?

We work closely with clients at every milestone to ensure satisfaction. If there's a genuine issue with deliverables not matching agreed specifications, we'll work to resolve it or provide proportional compensation.

Can I pause my project midway?

Yes, projects can be paused for up to 30 days. Extended pauses may require renegotiation of timelines and resource allocation.

What if I don't have all requirements ready?

We can start with a discovery phase to help you define requirements. This ensures we build exactly what you need.
